Photography

One of my favorite things to do when I'm out and about is take pictures of interesting things. I end up with a bunch of useless photos, but from time to time I take something that I'm really proud of.


I don't use any fancy equipment for my photography, just my iPhone. Why? I have a DSLR but it's just too bulky to take everywhere. I rarely take my DSLR anywhere unless I want to take really good photos. Otherwise, the 4k camera in my iPhone does just fine and it fits in my pocket!


I like to think back to an old proverb, "A bad worksman blames his tools." Sure, you can take better photos with an expensive camera, or you can adapt to the limitations of what you have and still take great photos.


In my opinion, the best camera is the one you already have.
